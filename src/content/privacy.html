Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 1, 2026

1. Information We Collect

AZ Games does not require you to create an account. We collect limited anonymous data through standard web analytics:

  • Pages visited and time spent
  • Browser type and operating system
  • Referring URL and approximate geographic region

We do not collect your name, email address, or any personally identifiable information unless you contact us voluntarily.

2. Cookies

We use cookies for analytics and to display relevant advertising (Google AdSense). You can disable cookies in your browser settings; this will not prevent you from playing games.

3. Third-Party Services

AZ Games uses the following third-party services that may set their own cookies:

  • Google Analytics — traffic analysis
  • Google AdSense — advertising

Game content is hosted on azgames.io and loaded inside an iframe. That service has its own privacy policy.

4. Data Retention

Analytics data is retained for up to 26 months and then automatically deleted.

5. Children's Privacy

AZ Games is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of any personal data we hold about you. Contact us at our contact page.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
